Case studies

Musifiy Mobile App

Role

UX Designer

Duration

5 weeks

Tools

Figma

Client

Personal Project

01.-

Context

Overview

Musify is an online music app which allow users to stream various online music arranged by specific themes such as genres, artist- centric playlists, decades, moods, and more. Such apps also filter the search results based on your needs, suggest music you might like, and are a great way to discover new music.

02.-

Research

02.-

Empathise

User Persona

To understand the Music App in a better way, We conducted two interviews to collect personas. This is to compare and understand the intended users and evaluating their pain points and needs to assist in deriving the initial ideation phase.

04.-

Define

Research Findings

The interview process has really opened us opportunity to identify the gap. Though both interviewees are different, both still poses a challenge to practise more accurate music app. The common challenge is not being able to share music, high subscription cost and very limited artist.

Problem statement

How might we make it easier for people to access it.

05.-

Ideate

Wireframing

We presented these ideas back to the user we have previously interviewed to collect feedbacks and improvement. The most common issue was they were not getting enough categories option to choose music from also they were not able to share lyrics.

It was then i decided to introduce lyrics sharing option from with in the app and added more categories to make it accessible for everyone.
